Important info about access to the building:

  • You must enter the building using the entrance on Bellefield Ave.
  • If you are not affiliated with CMU you will need to check in at the security desk prior to coming to the 4th floor. All in-person registrants will be added to the list to enter.
  • Alert rideshare to drop you at the Bellefield Ave. entrance, the building sits on the corner of Fifth and Bellefield Avenues.
